Companion Matching

The right companion makes
all the difference.

Connection isn't one-size-fits-all. That's why we offer thoughtful, preference-based matching — so every senior feels truly comfortable, safe, and understood.

Default Recommendation

Opposite-Gender
Companionship

Our default matching pairs seniors with a companion of the opposite gender. Research and lived experience show that cross-gender friendships often create a unique emotional openness — especially for seniors who have lost a spouse or partner. There's a natural ease, a warmth, a sense of being genuinely cared for that many find deeply comforting.

  • Creates a natural emotional ease and openness
  • Particularly supportive for those who've lost a spouse
  • Warm, friendly connection — never romantic
  • Clear ethical boundaries maintained at all times
  • Most requested and highly rated by our members
Available on Request

Same-Gender
Companionship

We fully honor every senior's comfort and preference. Same-gender companionship is available for anyone who feels more at ease, more open, or simply more themselves with someone of the same gender. There is no wrong choice — only the right fit for your loved one.

  • Available for any member, any plan, any time
  • No questions asked — comfort is all that matters
  • Same rigorous verification & training as all companions
  • Equally warm, genuine, and professionally supervised
  • Easily requested during signup or at any point after
🔬

Why gender-aware matching matters: Loneliness in seniors is often tied to the loss of specific types of relationships — a husband, a wife, a lifelong friend. By offering intentional companion matching rather than random assignment, we create conditions where real emotional connection is most likely to flourish. Every match is personal, considered, and always adjustable based on feedback.

Scam Protection

We monitor for every
common threat

Scammers exploit loneliness, grief, and trust. We replace that vulnerability with consistent human presence — and active, educated vigilance.

💌
Romance Scams
Emotional manipulation disguised as affection, designed to build trust before requesting money or personal information.
💻
Tech Support Scams
Fake pop-ups and urgent calls claiming to be from Microsoft, Apple, or other trusted brands.
🏦
Fake Bank Calls
Impersonators claiming fraud on accounts, pressuring seniors to move funds or reveal credentials.
📋
IRS & Government Fraud
Threats of legal action, arrest warrants, or tax debts designed to instill panic and extract payment.
📈
Investment Scams
Fraudulent "opportunities" promising guaranteed returns, targeting seniors' retirement savings.
🎰
Lottery & Inheritance Fraud
Fake winnings or unclaimed inheritances requiring fees or personal details to "claim."
