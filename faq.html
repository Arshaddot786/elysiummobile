The Service
Understanding Elysium Care

Elysium Care is a structured human companionship and scam protection service for seniors living alone. We provide daily warm contact — voice calls, text messages, and video calls — from verified, trained companions who genuinely care about your loved one's wellbeing. We are not a medical service, therapy clinic, or home care agency. We are a real human connection service, built to reduce loneliness, protect against scams, and make seniors feel valued every single day.

Primarily for seniors living alone — especially those who have lost a spouse, live far from family, or are at risk of social isolation and online scams. We also serve adults of any age experiencing loneliness, heartbreak, or scam trauma. Our services are available globally and can be arranged by adult children on behalf of a parent, or by individuals directly.

Completely real human beings. Every call, every text, every check-in is from a verified, trained Elysium Care companion — a real person with a real name who genuinely gets to know your loved one. We believe deeply that no algorithm or AI can replace genuine human warmth. That is the entire foundation of what we do.

Most call centers are scripted, transactional, and anonymous. Our companions are specifically matched to your loved one, build a genuine ongoing friendship over months and years, and are trained in scam awareness so they actively protect while connecting. We also offer community — group Zoom calls, movie nights, group chats where seniors make real friends. No call center offers that.

Safety & Trust
How we keep your loved one safe

Every companion goes through our multi-stage verification process: government ID verification, national criminal background check, sex offender registry check, multiple interview rounds, mandatory ethics training, and scam awareness certification. They are also supervised throughout their employment and reviewed regularly. No companion reaches a client without completing this entire process.

Healthy emotional attachment is actually the goal — that is what genuine friendship looks like. Our ethics training ensures companions maintain clear, appropriate boundaries. There is zero tolerance for romantic manipulation, financial solicitation, or any kind of secrecy. Supervisors monitor all companion activity and families receive regular reports. If anything ever feels off, you contact us immediately and we act immediately.

Absolutely not. This is our strictest policy and the reason we exist. Any companion who solicits money, gifts, or financial information from a client in any form is immediately terminated and reported. No exceptions. Our entire mission was born from our founder's personal experience with financial exploitation — this policy is not corporate language, it is a personal vow.

You can request a rematch at any time, for any reason, with no questions asked and no additional charge. Your loved one's comfort is always the priority. We will arrange a new companion match as quickly as possible — usually within 48 hours.

Companion Matching
Gender matching and preferences

Research and the lived experience of our clients consistently shows that cross-gender friendships often create a natural emotional openness — especially for seniors who have lost a spouse or partner. There is a unique warmth and ease that many seniors find comforting. However, this is always a preference — same-gender companionship is fully available and equally valued.

No. Unambiguously no. Our companionship is warm, friendly, and emotionally supportive — like a trusted friend. Any romantic language, behavior, or implication is strictly prohibited by our ethics policy and would result in immediate termination of the companion. The warmth of friendship and the manipulation of romance are completely different things, and our training ensures companions understand and respect that distinction completely.

Yes — and we actively try to match on language and cultural background where possible. Especially for expat communities and seniors whose first language is not English. Please note your language preferences when signing up and we will do our best to accommodate.

Scam Protection
How we protect your loved one every day

Through daily conversation and gentle ongoing education. Companions are trained in current scam tactics and weave awareness naturally into daily conversations — no alarm, no condescension, just consistent gentle guidance. They encourage members to check with them before sending money or personal information online. They become the trusted second opinion that a scammer cannot compete with.

Contact us immediately. For Premium Dignity Plan members, we offer immediate red-flag alert response. For all plan members, we will escalate this to a supervisor who will work with you and your family directly to assess the situation and help guide your loved one safely. We take active scam situations extremely seriously and respond urgently.

Community
The social world we create

Members have access to group Zoom calls, movie nights, trivia and games, group chat circles, birthday celebrations, and shared life storytelling sessions. Community access is included in the Companion & Protection and Premium Dignity plans. These group connections are often where the most meaningful friendships form — seniors meeting other seniors who truly understand what their life feels like right now.

We introduce community very gently — always through their companion first. No one is ever pushed into group participation. We follow each person's pace completely.
