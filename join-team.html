Our Hiring Philosophy

Broken people make
the best healers.

"I only hire people who have had their heart broken. Pain makes better companions than training ever could. You went through it. Now you help others through it."

— Arshad, Founder

Most companies hire for qualifications. We hire for humanity. A person who has sat in the dark at 3am and survived it — who has felt the weight of loneliness, grief, or betrayal — brings something to this work that no degree can teach.

We still train every companion rigorously. But the foundation we build on is not a CV. It is a heart that has been through something real — and came out on the other side softer, more patient, more genuinely caring because of it.

💔
Heartbreak Survivors
Those who lost themselves in love and rebuilt know exactly how to sit with someone in that same darkness.
🌑
Loneliness Survivors
Having felt truly alone makes you uniquely qualified to make someone else feel genuinely less alone.
💸
Scam Survivors
Those who carried the shame of being scammed — and survived it — bring zero judgment and total understanding.
🌱
Grief Survivors
Loss teaches patience, presence, and the rare ability to sit with someone's pain without trying to fix it immediately.
Who We're Looking For

Six qualities we value
above everything else

🤍
Genuine Kindness
Not performed kindness. Not professional kindness. The real kind — the kind that shows up even when no one is watching, and stays even when someone is difficult.
👂
The Ability to Truly Listen
Most people listen to respond. We need people who listen to understand. The seniors and clients we serve need to feel heard — not handled.
⏰
Consistency & Reliability
Our clients depend on showing up. For lonely and vulnerable people, a missed call is not a small thing. We need people who understand that dependability is care.
🚫
Zero Judgment
Our clients come to us after being judged, embarrassed, or shamed. We need people who have been through enough to know that judgment helps absolutely no one.
🛡️
Strong Personal Ethics
Our ethics policy is non-negotiable. No financial solicitation. No emotional manipulation. No secrecy. We need people who hold these values naturally — not as rules, but as character.
🌍
Cultural Openness
Our clients come from every background, every religion, every country. We need companions who see every human being as worthy of the same warmth regardless of where they come from.
